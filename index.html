Researcher
Bytedance AI Lab
Email: zhouhao.nlpbytedance.com , haozhou0806gmail.com

Hello! I am a researcher at Bytedance AI lab, working on natural language processing. Before that, I recieved my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2017. My research interests are machine learning and its applications in natural language processing. Currently, I focus on deep generative models for natural language generation.
